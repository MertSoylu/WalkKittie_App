**WalkKittie** is a virtual pet application I developed that transforms your daily activities (walking and drinking water) into a fun game.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🚶‍♂️ Step Counter & Activity Tracker
*   **Fully Automated:** No need to keep the app open; it counts your steps in the background using your phone's sensors.
*   **Low Battery Consumption:** Built with an optimized structure to ensure minimal battery usage.
*   **Goal Tracking:** Easily track your daily steps and calories burned.

### 💧 Water Intake Tracker
*   Set your daily water intake goal and log every glass you drink.

### 🐱 Your Feline Friend
*   **Needs:** Your pet gets hungry, tired, and bored.
    *   🍖 **Hunger:** Buy food using points earned from your steps.
    *   ⚡ **Energy:** Playing makes your cat tired; let them sleep to recover energy.
    *   ❤️ **Happiness:** If neglected, your pet becomes sad.
*   **Customization:** Give your pet any name you like!

### 🛒 Shop
*   A newly designed separate Shop screen where you can spend your rewards on food and surprise items for your cat.

### 🎮 Playroom (Mini-Games)
With a completely revamped, spacious design, the playroom offers 5 different games to earn extra points:
1.  **🎰 Lucky Wheel:** Test your luck.
2.  **🧠 Memory:** Test your memory by matching cards.
3.  **⚡ Reflex:** Measure your speed by catching paws as they appear.
4.  **✂️ Rock-Paper-Scissors:** A classic game where you face off against your cat.
5.  **🧺 Catch! :** Swipe the basket to collect falling rewards and avoid bombs!

## 🛠️ Technical Details

This project was developed to learn and implement modern Android technologies:

*   **Language:** Kotlin
*   **UI:** Jetpack Compose (Material 3 Design, Advanced Animations)
*   **Database:** Room & DataStore (For local persistence)
*   **Architecture:** MVVM & Clean Architecture (For organized and maintainable code)
*   **Dependency Injection:** Hilt
*   **Background Jobs:** WorkManager & Coroutines (For periodic reminders and state updates)
